Impact of Computers

Computers have transformed various aspects of human life, from communication and education to business and entertainment. The internet, made possible by computers, has revolutionized global connectivity, enabling instant communication and access to vast amounts of information. In the business world, computers have automated processes, increased efficiency, and opened up new avenues for commerce through e-commerce platforms. Furthermore, the entertainment industry has been reshaped by computers, with digital media and streaming services becoming ubiquitous.
