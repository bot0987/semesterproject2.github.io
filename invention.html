Invention of Computers

The history of computers dates back to the 19th century with pioneers like Charles Babbage and Ada Lovelace laying the groundwork for modern computing. The invention of the first electronic digital computer, ENIAC, in the 1940s marked a significant milestone in computer history. Subsequent developments, such as the transistor and integrated circuit, paved the way for the miniaturization and commercialization of computers.
